FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know. Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out to our support team.

Is there really a free plan?
Yes! Our Starter plan is completely free and includes up to 1,000 events per month, 3 dashboards, and 7 days of data retention. No credit card required to sign up.
How does the 14-day trial work?
When you sign up for Pro or Enterprise, you get 14 days of full access at no cost. If you decide it's not for you, just cancel before the trial ends — you won't be charged.
Can I switch plans later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences automatically.
What integrations do you support?
We support 50+ integrations including Slack, GitHub, Jira, HubSpot, Salesforce, Google Analytics, Stripe, and many more. We also offer a REST API and webhooks for custom integrations.
Is my data secure?
Security is our top priority. We're SOC 2 Type II compliant, all data is encrypted at rest and in transit, and we offer SSO and SAML for enterprise customers. We never sell your data.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days of a paid plan, contact our support team for a full refund — no questions asked.
